Instuctions and Information About operators

Operator Name Information
+ Addition For adding two numbers
eg : 2+3 = 5
- Substraction For substracting second number from the first one
eg : 5-3 = 2
* Multiplication For Multipling two numbers
eg : 5*2 = 10
/ Division For Diving Two Numbers and It will give Quotient in float format
// Floor Division For Diving Two Numbers and It will give Quotient in int format
** Exponentiation For raising a number B to power A like A^B
eg : 5**2 = 25 (square of 5 is 25)
% Modulus It will give the remainder
eg : 5%2 = 1
